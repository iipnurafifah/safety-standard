Policy Statement

Our application maintains strict safety standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a safe environment for all users, particularly minors, and have implemented comprehensive measures to prevent, detect, and report any content or behavior related to child sexual abuse and exploitation.

Safety Measures
  • Zero-tolerance policy for CSAE content
  • Advanced content moderation systems
  • User reporting mechanisms
  • Cooperation with law enforcement authorities
  • Regular safety audits and updates
